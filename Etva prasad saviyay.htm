tva prasad | sayvi-aa |
sravag sudh samuh sidhaan ke 
dekh phirio ghar jog jati ke |
sur surardan sudh sudhadik sant 
samuh anek mati ke |
sare hi des ko dekh raheo mat 
kou na dekhiat pranpati ke |
sri bhagvan ki bhai kripa hu 
te ek rati bin ek rati ke |1|21|

mate matang jare jar sang anup 
utang surang savare |
kot turang kurang se kudat paun 
ke gaun ko jaat nivaare |
bhari bhujaan ke bhuup bhali 
bidh niavat sis n jaat beechare |
aete bhaye tu kaha bhaye 
bhupat ant ko naange hi paai padhare |2|22|

jeet phire sabh des disaan ko 
bajat dhol mridang nagaare |
gunjat gudd gajaan ke sundar 
hinsat hai hayraaj hajaare |
bhut bhavikh bhavaan ke bhupat 
kaun gane nahin jaat bichaare |
sri pat sri bhagvan bhaje bin 
ant kou ant ke dham sidhaare |3|23|

teerath naan dayaa dam daan su 
sanjam nem anek bisekhe |
baed puraan kateb kuraan jameen 
jamaan sabhan ke pekhe |
poun ahaar jati jathdaar sabai 
su beechaar hajar ke dekhe |
sri bhagvan bhaje bin bhoopat 
ek rati bin ek na lekhe |4|24|

sudh sipaha durant dubaaha so 
saaj samaha durjaan dalenge |
bhaari guman bhare man me kar 
parbat pankh hale na halenge |
tor areen maror mahasan mate 
matangan maan malenge |
sri pat sri bhagvan kripa bin 
tyaag jahaan nidhan chalenge|5|25|

beer apaar bade bariyaaar abhee-chaaray 
saar ki dhaar bachaiya |
torat des malind mahasan mate 
gajan ke maan malaiya |
gade gadaan ko todanhaar so baatan 
hi chak chaar lavaiya |
sahib sri sab ko sir nayak jachak 
anek so ek divaayiya |6|26|

daanav dev fanind nisaachar bhut 
bavikh bhavaan japenge |
jeev jite jal me thal me pal hi 
pal me sab thaap khapenge |
pun pratapan baad jet dhun paapan 
ke bhau punj khapenge |
saadh samuha prasann phire jag 
shatra sabhaay avalok chapaaynge |7|27|

maanav indra gajindra naaraadhip 
joaun trilok ko raaj karenge |
kot isnaan gajadik daan anek suambar saaj barenge |
bramha mahesur bisan sachipat 
ant phasaay jam phaas parenge |
je nar sri pat ke pras hai 
pag te nar pher na deh dharenge |8|28|

kaha bhayon jo do lochan mond ke 
baith raheyo bak dhyaan lagayo |
nhaat phirio le saat samundran lok 
gayo parlok gavayo |
baas kiyo bikhiyaan so baith ke 
aise hi aise so baes bitaaye |
saach kaho sun leho sabhaay jin prem 
kiyo tin hi prabh payoo |9|29|

kahu le pahan puuj dharyeo sir kahu 
le ling gare latkayon |
kahu lakhiyo har aavachee disaa main 
kahu pachaa ko sees nivaayon |
kou bhutaan ko poojat hai pass 
kouu mritaan ko poojan dhaayo |
kurr kriya urjhiyo sabh hi jag 
sri bhagvan ko bhed na paayo |10|20|
